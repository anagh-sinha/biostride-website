Meet Our Founder

The vision, journey, and mission driving Bio-Stride's innovation in emergency diagnostics

Anagh Sinha

Founder & CEO, Bio-Stride

A dual-degree holder in Electronics Engineering and Biological Sciences from BITS Pilani, Anagh combines deep technical expertise with personal conviction. His journey from a small town in Rajasthan to leading research at the University of Tokyo and ETH Zurich reflects an unwavering commitment to solving healthcare's most pressing challenges.

Driven by personal experiences with family members affected by delayed TBI diagnosis, Anagh founded Bio-Stride to ensure that geography and infrastructure never determine healthcare outcomes. His vision: democratize access to life-saving diagnostics across India's 1.4 billion population.

The Journey

2002-2019
Early Years: Curiosity Meets Purpose
Born in Gangapur City, Rajasthan, to a family of educators. Mother, a government school teacher, instilled the value of asking "why" and serving the community. Witnessed healthcare inequities firsthand as family members struggled with inadequate medical infrastructure.
2019-2021
BITS Pilani: Foundation & First Ventures
Pursued dual degree in Electronics Engineering and Biological Sciences. Founded Oxon during COVID-19 recovery, addressing waste management for 2 million people. Built and exited DermaGlow, learning crucial lessons about customer-centric innovation.
2021-2023
Global Research Excellence
Received prestigious Amgen Research Fellowship and IIE Invent Award. Conducted award-winning research at University of Tokyo (with Nobel laureate team) and ETH Zurich (#1 in Europe). Won Best Poster Award at Tokyo International Conference for diagnostic systems research.
2023-2024
The Catalyst: Personal Tragedy to Innovation
Experienced 6-hour CT scan wait at AIIMS Delhi after fainting. Combined with memories of grandmother, cousin, and uncle becoming permanently disabled from delayed TBI diagnosis. Started thesis research on electrochemical detection at BITS Pilani's tumor microenvironment lab.
2025-Present
Bio-Stride: From Lab to Life
Founded Bio-Stride to commercialize validated electrochemical TBI detection technology. Conducted 50+ problem discovery interviews with healthcare professionals. Building team of experts from BITS, AIIMS, and IISER. Patent pending for portable diagnostic system.
