Everything you need to know about Bio-Stride TBI diagnostics

Bio-Stride demonstrates 97.6% sensitivity and 95.2% specificity, matching or exceeding CT scan accuracy for TBI detection. Clinical validation shows our biomarker approach detects brain injury at the cellular level, often before structural damage visible on CT scans.
The device detects specific biomarkers (GFAP and UCH-L1) released into blood when brain cells are damaged. Using advanced electrochemical sensors, it measures these proteins with laboratory-grade precision in a portable format.
